AI for Agriculture

Artificial Intelligence is transforming agriculture by helping farmers make better decisions using data.

Machine learning models can analyze soil nutrients, rainfall patterns, and temperature to recommend the most suitable crops for planting. This improves productivity while reducing environmental impact.

Through my crop recommendation project, I explored how data science can support sustainable agriculture. Using Python and machine learning, I built a model that predicts suitable crops based on soil conditions.

AI can also support:

  • Precision farming
  • Pest detection using computer vision
  • Weather prediction models
  • Yield optimization

As someone with a background in Agricultural Biotechnology, I am interested in how AI can support resilient food systems and improve farmer livelihoods.
