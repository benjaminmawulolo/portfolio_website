Data Storytelling

Data storytelling is the ability to communicate insights clearly using data, visuals, and context.

In many development projects, valuable data exists but is not always communicated effectively. By combining data visualization with clear messaging, we can support better decision-making.

My background in communications helps me translate complex analysis into messages that stakeholders can understand easily.

Strong data storytelling includes:

  • Clear visuals
  • Simple explanations
  • Relevant context
  • Actionable insights

Through analytics projects, I aim to connect data insights with real-world impact in agriculture, education, and development.
